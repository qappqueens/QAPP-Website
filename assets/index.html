Why Join QAPP?


Gain Real-World Experience


QAPP gives its team members the opportunity to develop the specific skills that are vital within any workplace, such as the ability to work in large and small groups, and the ability to improve written and verbal communication skills. In this regard, QAPP not only allows its team members to work with a diverse group of students from all faculties, but also gives team members the ability to create a more enticing resume altogether.

Develop Incredible Apps


QAPP is focused on creation. Initially, every team member is encouraged think of, and present their ideas for innovative apps. These apps are criticized and evaluated through a comprehensive scoring system developed by club executives. Once an idea is decided on, each department dedicates its time to making the app as successful as possible.

Meet and work with Amazing People!


One of the unique aspects of QAPP is that it allows a group of students to work on one project simultaneously, allowing team members to build off one another. Although there are different departments within the club, ranging from a graphics design team, to an android development team, each team member shares the same common goal; To create the best app possible.
